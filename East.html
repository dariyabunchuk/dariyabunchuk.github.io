Whiteface Mountain, New York

The 5th highest mountain in the Adirondacks, Whiteface delivers the greatest vertical of any lift-serviced mountain in the Northeast, with mile after mile of impeccably groomed cruising trails, 98% snowmaking coverage, a 5-star ski and snowboard school.

Whiteface delivers on the promise of a great ski experience for every level. A relaxed learning environment for beginners, lots of options for the family, and the most spectacular and varied advanced and expert terrain in the East. Check out the specs below.
